Internship Assignments

Welcome to my internship project at NullClass. Here are the details of my assignments.

Assignments Overview

  1. Average Engagement Rate & Impressions

    Create a visual that shows the average engagement rate and total impressions for tweets posted between '01-01-2020' and '30-06-2020'. Filter out tweets that received fewer than 100 impressions and where likes are 0. The graph should only be shown for tweets posted between 3 PM to 5 PM IST.

  2. Top 10 Tweets by Retweets & Likes

    Build a chart to identify the top 10 tweets by the sum of retweets and likes, filtering out tweets posted on weekends, only displaying between 3 PM to 6 PM for tweets with even impressions, odd dates, and below 30 words.

  3. Clustered Bar Chart of Clicks

    Create a clustered bar chart that breaks down the sum of URL clicks, user profile clicks, and hashtag clicks by tweet category, only including tweets with interactions. This should be displayed between 3 PM to 6 PM, with even dates and below 40 words.

  4. Dual-Axis Chart for Media Interactions

    Show the number of media views and engagements by the day of the week for the last quarter. Highlight significant spikes, shown between 3 PM to 6 PM, with even impressions and odd dates below 30 words.

  5. Engagement Rate Comparison

    Analyze tweets for engagement rate comparisons between those with app opens and those without, posted between 9 AM to 5 PM on weekdays, shown from 12 PM to 6 PM, with even impressions, odd dates, and below 40 words.
